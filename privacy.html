Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What this app does

Karuta Ad Maker is a free browser-based tool that helps Karuta Discord bot players generate trade advertisements from their card collection data.

Data we collect

We collect no personal data. Specifically:

  • Your CSV spreadsheet file is read entirely in your browser and never uploaded to any server.
  • No account, login, or registration is required.
  • We do not store your card data, Discord username, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No cookies are set by this application.

How your data is used

Your CSV spreadsheet is read entirely in your browser and never sent anywhere. All ad templates are generated locally on your device using your card data. No card information, usernames, or any other data is transmitted to external servers.

Advertising

This site may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-party services

This app loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). Google may collect limited data as part of this service — see Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

This service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out via the project's GitHub repository.
